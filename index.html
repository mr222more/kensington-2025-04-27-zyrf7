About Kensington

Founded in 1995, Kensington emerged from a passion for combining innovative design with superior craftsmanship. Our journey began in a small workshop in Beverly Hills, where we pioneered the use of premium materials and cutting-edge technology in custom shower enclosures.

Today, we remain committed to our core values of excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Our mission is to transform ordinary bathrooms into extraordinary spaces, delivering unparalleled quality and service to each client while pushing the boundaries of design and functionality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty coverage for your products?

We offer a lifetime warranty on all glass and hardware against manufacturing defects, plus a 10-year warranty on our ClearShield coating.

How long does installation typically take?

Most standard installations are completed within one day. Custom projects may require 1-2 days depending on complexity.

What maintenance is required?

With our ClearShield protection, simple weekly cleaning with a mild glass cleaner is sufficient. No harsh chemicals needed.

Can you match existing bathroom fixtures?

Yes, we offer a wide range of hardware finishes to complement your existing fixtures and design elements.

What is the typical lead time for custom orders?

Custom orders typically require 2-3 weeks from final measurement to installation, ensuring perfect fit and finish.
